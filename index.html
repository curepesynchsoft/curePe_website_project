Our Products



An integrated App to serve you in times of your healthcare needs. Out of pocket settlement of healthcare expenses in India is as high as 62 percent compared to 11 percent in the US and 36 percent in China. To reduce this gap and prevent people from falling into poverty and debt trap, we have built a platform which will act as a powerful tool to help you in your (+family) healthcare expense management as well as enable you to avail various healthcare services hasslefree and worryfree.
Our platform provides:

  •  Analysis of your/family members vitals and suggest preventive steps.
  •  Suggest best insurance as per vitals analysis.
  •  Analysis of your existing insurance plans to check if it is sufficient to get you/family members best in class treatment.
  •  Provide healthcare emergency loans to meet out of pocket expenses not covered in Insurance.
